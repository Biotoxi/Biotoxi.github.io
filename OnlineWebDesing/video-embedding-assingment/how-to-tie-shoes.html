Learn how to tie your shoes!

This article will shoe you the step by step procces in order to be a master at tieing your shoes!

Step 1:

In order to start your shoe tying adventure you need to have your laces in each hand. Below is an image that shows you how you should be holding your shoe laces.

Step 2:

Now that you have Step one mastered we can move onto the second step! Crossing the laces. Shown in the picture below is how your laces should appear.

Step 3:

Now that you have crossed the road called Step two we can move into some deeper waters! With your right hand you are going to want to thread the shoe lace through the hole that appears just above the tounge of the shoe and bring it up so it looks like the lace is twisted around the other lace. Below is how your laces should look. If not take a step back and try again!

Step 4:

Now that your laces are twisted its time to give the laces some bunny ears and cross the ears!. Below is how your laces should look at this point in your shoe tieing extravaganza!

Step 5:

Now that your little bunny is all crossed up its time to take the entirety of the ear in your right hand and wrap it around the left ear just like what we did in step three! Below is how your laces should look like.

Step 6:

Now that you have your jumbled mess of laces its time to pull the ears on a bunny. Take each ear and pull them away from each other and your shoes will be tied!. Below is how they should look. if not reread this article and try again until you achive your goal!
