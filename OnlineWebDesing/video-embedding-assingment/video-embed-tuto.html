Learn how to embed a video!

This article will show you the steps to embed a video to your website!

Before we get started let's talk about what the types of embedding are. There are a few ways to embed a video and the easiest would be to upload your video to YouTube and right-click on the video after its uploaded and pressing the "Copy embed code" button. Otherwise you can manually write the code with the video tag.

Step 1: Going to Youtube

To start your embedding adventure you need to have your video in hand. Go to YouTube and search for your video in the search bar or upload it to YouTube itself.

Step 2: Obtaining the code

Now that you have your video right-click on the video and a black box with different options should show up, click on the option that says "Copy embed code"

Step 3:

Now that you have the code copied to your clipboard you can paste it into your editor of choice

Step 4:

Now that it's in your code you can just upload your code and the video should be embedded.
