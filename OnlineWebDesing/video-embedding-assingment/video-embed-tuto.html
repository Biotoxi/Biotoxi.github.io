Learn how to embed a video!

This article will show you the steps in order to embed a video to your website!

Before we get started lets talk about what the types of embeding are. There are a few ways to embed a video and the easist would be to upload your video to youtube and right click on the video after its uploaded and pressing the "Copy embed code" button. Otherwise you can manually write the code with the video tag.

Step 1: Going to Youtube

In order to start your embedding adventure you need to have your video in hand. Go to Youtube and search for your video in the search bar or upload it to Youtube itself.

Step 2: Obtaining the code

Now that you have your video right click on the video and a black box with diffrent options should show up, click on the option that say "Copy embed code"
